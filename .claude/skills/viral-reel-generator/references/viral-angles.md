# Viral Angle Finder

Transform boring topics into viral concepts using these frames.

## 1. The "Villain" Angle
*   **Concept:** Identify a common enemy (an industry, a habit, a tool).
*   **Structure:** "Why [Industry] is lying to you about [Topic]."
*   **Example:** *Topic: SEO.* -> *Angle: Why Google hates your AI content.*

## 2. The "Counter-Intuitive" Angle
*   **Concept:** Take a widely held belief and prove the opposite.
*   **Structure:** "Stop doing [Good Habit]. It's actually [Bad Result]."
*   **Example:** *Topic: Productivity.* -> *Angle: Why waking up at 5AM is destroying your focus.*

## 3. The "Curated List" Angle
*   **Concept:** Save the viewer time by aggregating value.
*   **Structure:** "I tested 50 [Tools] so you don't have to."
*   **Example:** *Topic: AI Tools.* -> *Angle: I paid for every AI wrapper. These are the only 3 worth it.*

## 4. The "David vs. Goliath" Angle
*   **Concept:** How a small entity outsmarted a big one.
*   **Structure:** "How this one [Person/Small Biz] beat [Giant Corporation]."
*   **Example:** *Topic: Marketing.* -> *Angle: How a $0 budget campaign beat Nike.*
