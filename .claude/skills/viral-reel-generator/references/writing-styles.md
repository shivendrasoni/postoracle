# Script Writing Styles & Anti-Slop Rules

Choose a personality profile based on the content density.

## Style A: "The Punchy" (Trends, News, Quick Tips)
**One-line identity:** High-energy, pattern-interrupt explainer that hooks in 2 seconds.

**Rhythm:**
*   Short. Short. Medium explanation. Short punch.
*   No wasted words.
*   Cuts happen every 2-3 seconds.

**Signature Moves:**
*   **Mid-action opens:** "Zero damage. 30 bullets." (No "So there was a...")
*   **Dry wit:** Understate the punchline.
*   **One-word transitions:** "But." / "Why?" / "Because."

## Style B: "The Deep Dive" (Educational, Technical, Behind-the-Scenes)
**One-line identity:** The "How It Works" nerd who roasts lazy solutions.

**Structure:**
1.  **Hook:** Bold technical claim.
2.  **Context:** "See, most [people/competitors] do [Lazy Way]..."
3.  **Mechanism:** "...But [Subject] uses [Specific Technique]." (Explain HOW).
4.  **Escalation:** "But here's where it gets insane."
5.  **Payoff:** The final result.

**Signature Moves:**
*   **The Connector:** Use "See," "Meaning," and "Therefore" to glue sentences.
*   **The Contrast:** Always compare the "Lazy Way" vs. the "Smart Way."
*   **The Escalation:** Always have a moment where the complexity doubles ("But it gets worse").

## THE ANTI-SLOP RULES (Mandatory)

**If a script contains these, it is failed AI slop.**

### 1. NO Three-Word Repetitive Patterns
*    "Fast. Scalable. Secure."
*    "Bold. Brave. Beautiful."
*    **Fix:** "The system is fast and scalable, making security automatic."

### 2. NO "Question? Answer." Listings
*    "Hotel? Trivago. Rates? Low."
*    "The problem? Time. The solution? AI."
*    **Fix:** "The problem was time, so they solved it with AI."

### 3. NO Generic Transitions
*    "Let's dive in."
*    "Here's the kicker."
*    "What's up guys."
*    "In this video..."
*    **Fix:** Just say the next sentence. Use "But" or "See."

### 4. NO Imaginary Scenarios
*    "Imagine you are a knight..."
*    "Picture this: You're walking..."
*    **Fix:** Describe what *actually* happens. "When a knight walks..."

### 5. NO Hype Adjectives
*    "Mind-blowing," "Game-changing," "Revolutionary."
*    **Fix:** Use facts. "This changed the industry standard overnight."
